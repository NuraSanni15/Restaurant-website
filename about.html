About us

We are pleased to welcome you to Sweet Retreat! An Italian Restaurant based in Abuja, the heart and capital of Nigeria. Our menu is filled with an eclectic selection of Food & Wine, guaranteed to leave an impression on our customers. Sweet Retreat is a family owned business, gaining a great reputation for its Cleanliness, Warm Ambience and ultimately superb Dinning Experience. The staff is comprised of individuals from all across the world, giving you a diverse group of people to learn and socialise with. Whether you are in search of a quick breakfast or lunch, a relaxing dinner, or an appetiser and cocktails, our experienced and courteous staff will make your visit a memorable one.
